Privacy Policy

Ready Mart Shop  ·  Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Ready Mart Shop is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account: Name, email, phone number
  • Profile: Photo and contact details
  • Transactions: Add Money and Withdrawal data
  • Orders & Products: Items you add or manage
  • Camera: Used only when uploading payment screenshots
  • Device: Model and OS version (for performance)
  • Support: Messages via Help & Support or Chatbot

2. How We Use It

  • Account creation, login, and OTP verification
  • Processing transactions and managing orders
  • Providing customer support and chatbot assistance

3. Camera Permission

Camera access is requested only when you upload a payment proof image. We never access your camera otherwise.

4. Data Security & Sharing

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard protection. We do not sell or share your data except when required by law or with trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data

  • Request account deletion by contacting us

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
