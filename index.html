Privacy Policy

App Name: Ready Mart Shop

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Ready Mart Shop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Info: Name, email address, phone number during Registration and Login
  • OTP Verification: Phone number for OTP-based login
  • Profile Info: Profile name, photo, and contact details
  • Financial Info: Add Money and Withdrawal transaction data
  • Product & Order Data: Products you add and orders you manage
  • Camera & Images: Camera permission is used only when you upload payment screenshots for Add Money
  • Device Info: Device model, OS version for app performance improvement
  • Support Data: Messages sent via Help & Support or Chatbot

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify your identity via OTP
  • To process Add Money and Withdrawal requests
  • To manage your Products and Orders
  • To provide Help & Support and Chatbot assistance
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send important notifications about your account

3. Camera Permission

We request Camera access only for the purpose of uploading payment proof images during the Add Money process. We do not access your camera at any other time without your explicit action.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To trusted service providers who assist in app operations under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Forgot Password

When you use the Forgot Password feature, we use your registered email or phone number to verify your identity and allow you to reset your password securely.

7. Chatbot & FAQ

Messages sent through our Chatbot or FAQ section may be stored to improve our support services. No personally identifiable information is shared externally from these interactions.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your profile information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw camera permission anytime from device settings

9. Children's Privacy

Ready Mart Shop is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
